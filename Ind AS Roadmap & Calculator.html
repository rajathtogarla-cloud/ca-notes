Key Implementation Rules

General Application

Irreversibility: Once IND AS is applied (voluntarily or mandatorily), it must be used for all subsequent years, even if criteria are no longer met.

Comparatives: First-time adopters must restate previous year's figures.
Example: Applying for FY 2017-18? Comparatives are for 2016-17. Transition Date: 01.04.2016.

Net Worth Calculation

Net Worth = [A] - [B]

[A] Add: Paid-up Capital, Reserves & Surplus (Gen Res, P&L, Sec Prem, Free Res, ESOP, Cap Res-Promoter/Grant)
[B] Less: Acc. Losses, Def. Exp, Misc. Exp, Fictitious Assets
Exclude: Revaluation Reserve, Cap Res (Amalgamation), Calls in arrears, Share application money, Warrants.

Listing Criteria

  • Applies if Equity/Debt is listed (or in process) on any recognized stock exchange (India/Abroad).
  • Status checked on 31.03.
  • Exemption: Small companies (Paid-up < ₹25 Cr) listed on SME/ITP platforms without IPO.

Group Companies (Chain Rule)

Chain Applicability: If IND AS applies to ONE company, it applies to ALL (Holding + Subsidiary + Associate + JV). Applies to Standalone & Consolidated.

Foreign Subsidiaries: Keep local accounts but prepare IND AS financials for Indian parent consolidation.

Different Standards: If Holding & Subsidiary differ (one AS, one Ind AS), Subsidiary prepares extra set for consolidation matching Holding.

Timing: Relationship must exist at initial applicability date. Sold shares prior? Rule doesn't apply.

Sector Specifics

  • NBFCs/Banks/Insurance: NO voluntary adoption. Must wait for mandate. RBI relaxations ≠ Exemption.
  • Mutual Funds: Mandatory from 1st April 2023.
  • Section 8 (Non-Profit): No exemption. Must follow if criteria met.
