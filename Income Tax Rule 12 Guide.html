Income Tax Rule 12

Assessment Procedure & Returns

Return of Income & Fringe Benefits

A comprehensive guide to Forms ITR-1 to ITR-7, eligibility criteria, and the prescribed manner of furnishing returns as per Income Tax Rule 12.

Applicable for Assessment Year 2024-25 onwards

Form SAHAJ (ITR-1)

For Individuals (Resident & OR)

The most common form for salaried individuals. Applicable if total income includes:

Salaries / Pension

Income from Salary or Family Pension.

House Property

Income from ONE house property (excluding brought forward losses).

Other Sources

Interest etc. (Excluding lottery/race horse winnings).

Agriculture

Agricultural income up to ₹5,000.

Who CANNOT file ITR-1?

  • Assets or financial interest outside India
  • Signing authority in foreign bank account
  • Income from sources outside India
  • Income apportionable u/s 5A
  • Deduction claimed u/s 57 (other than 57(iia))
  • Director in a company
  • Held unlisted equity shares during the year
  • Income assessable in another person’s hands (TDS in others’ name)
  • Relief claimed u/s 90 / 90A or deduction u/s 91
  • Agricultural income exceeding ₹5,000
  • Total income exceeding ₹50 lakhs
  • Dividend income taxable u/s 115BBDA
  • Income taxable u/s 115BBE
  • Cash withdrawals subject to TDS u/s 194N
  • Tax payment or TDS deferred on ESOPs

ITR-2

Individuals & HUF (No Business Income)

Applicable for Individuals and HUFs not having income from Profits and Gains of Business or Profession.

Covered Scenarios

Capital Gains, More than one house property, Foreign Assets/Income, Director in company, Unlisted shares, Income > 50L.

Ineligibility

Cannot be used if you have any income under the head "Business or Profession".

ITR-3

Individuals & HUF (With Business Income)

The comprehensive form for Individuals and HUFs having income from Business or Profession.

Business Owners / Partners

Proprietors, Professionals, or Partners in a firm.

Catch-all

Use this if you are not eligible for ITR-1, ITR-2, or ITR-4.

Form SUGAM (ITR-4)

Presumptive Taxation Scheme

Simple Calculation

Who can file?

  • Individuals (Resident & OR)
  • Hindu Undivided Families (HUF)
  • Firms (excluding LLPs)
Presumptive Sections

Income computed under special provisions:

  • Section 44AD (Business)
  • Section 44ADA (Professionals)
  • Section 44AE (Goods Carriages)

Exclusions (Cannot File ITR-4)

  • Assets or financial interest located outside India.
  • Signing authority in any bank account located outside India.
  • Income from any source outside India.
  • Income apportionable in accordance with section 5A.
  • Director in a company or holder of unlisted equity shares.
  • Total income exceeding ₹50 lakh.
  • Ownership of more than one house property.
  • Brought forward or carry-forward loss under any head of income.
  • Income assessable in another person’s hands where TDS is deducted.
  • Relief claimed under sections 90 or 90A or deduction claimed under section 91.
  • Agricultural income exceeding ₹5,000.
  • Dividend income taxable under section 115BBDA.
  • Income taxable under section 115BBE.
  • ESOP perquisite with deferred tax payment or deduction.

ITR-5

For persons other than:

  • Individual
  • HUF
  • Company
  • Person filing ITR-7
Includes Firms, LLPs, AOPs, BOIs.

ITR-6

For Companies.

Excluding companies claiming exemption under section 11 (Charitable/Religious purposes).

Must be filed electronically.

ITR-7

For persons including companies required to file return under:

  • 139(4A) - Charitable/Religious Trust
  • 139(4B) - Political Party
  • 139(4C) - Scientific Research Inst.
  • 139(4D) - University/College

Note on Attachments & Audit Reports

The return shall not be accompanied by attachments (computation, tax proof, etc.). However, Audit Reports (e.g., u/s 44AB, 10(23C), 80-IA, 92E) must be furnished electronically.

Rule 12(3): Manner of Furnishing Return

How you must submit your return based on your category.

Person Category Condition Manner of Furnishing
Individual or HUF Accounts required to be audited u/s 44AB Digital Signature Electronic Verification Code (EVC)
Super Senior Citizen (80+ years) furnishing ITR-1 or ITR-4 Digital Signature Electronic Verification Code Web Submit + ITR-V Paper Form (Physical)
In any other case Digital Signature Electronic Verification Code Web Submit + ITR-V
Company All cases Electronically under Digital Signature ONLY
Person filing ITR-7 Political Party Digital Signature
Other ITR-7 filers Digital Signature EVC ITR-V
Firm / LLP / Others (ITR-5) Audit required u/s 44AB Digital Signature
Other cases Digital Signature EVC ITR-V
