What is TDS?

TDS is a system where tax is deducted at the time of earning or payment of income, rather than waiting for the tax return. It ensures the Government receives revenue throughout the year ("Pay as you earn").

Why TDS Exists?

  • Early collection of tax
  • Regular cash flow to Govt
  • Reduces tax evasion
  • Widens tax base

Why is Form 26AS Critical?

Maintained by the IT Department, this form is the final proof of tax credit. If your TDS does not appear here, you cannot claim credit in your ITR.
