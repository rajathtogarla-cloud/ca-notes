What is TCS?

TCS is a system where tax is collected by the seller from the buyer at the time of sale of specified goods or services. Unlike TDS (deduction from payment), this is an addition to the sale value.

Why TCS Exists?

  • Tracking high-value txns
  • Widening tax base
  • Prevents turnover suppression
  • Early tax collection

The Buyer's Proof

TCS is treated as tax paid on your behalf. It must appear in Form 26AS for you (the buyer) to claim credit in your Income Tax Return. It is NOT an additional tax, but an advance tax.
